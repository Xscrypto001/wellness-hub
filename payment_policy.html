Important Refund Policy

When you cancel a booking, 10% of the total amount is deducted as a processing fee. The remaining 90% is refunded to your original payment method.

Cancellation Windows

1

24+ Hours Before Appointment

Full refund (minus 10% processing fee). No questions asked.

2

2-24 Hours Before Appointment

50% refund of service cost + 90% refund of any pre-paid add-ons.

3

Less Than 2 Hours Before

No refund available. Rescheduling options may be offered.
